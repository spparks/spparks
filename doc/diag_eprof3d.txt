app_style command :h3

[Syntax:]

app_style style args :pre

style = application style name
args = args :ul

[Examples:]

app_style ising 100 100
app_style potts 1000 1000 4 :pre

[Description:]

diag_style	eprof3d 0.1 axis x filename eprof3d.dat

If the boundary option is provided, the average energy is provided as
a function of distance from the nearest sector boundary.  In this
case, the overall average energy and the average energy immediately to
the left and right of the sector boundary is used for stats output.

diag_style	eprof3d 0.1 filename eprof3d.dat boundary
